Why Retain a Precast Engineer?

At Safe Roads Engineering (SRE), we transform your visions into concrete realities.

Our structural engineers are your strategic allies in the world of precast operations, and we will work with you to develop your in-house staff, act as experts on behalf of your company, and provide prompt and reliable service that will go above and beyond your expectations.

We currently offer two retainer service models to choose from:

Fixed / Flexible Budget

With this model, you will determine the annual budget, benefit from a discounted engineering service rate for billable hours, and receive a monthly invoice that can be fixed or adjusted as needed.

On-Demand

With the on-demand model, you will receive a monthly bill that is based on the actual number of hours used, all calculated at our preferred retained engineering service rate.

Services Offered

Technical Assessments

We will provide technical opinions on new projects, design / manufacturing challenges, quality issues, and installation recommendations.

Structural Designs

We offer structural designs of precast concrete structures and components.

Manufacturing Support

You will receive technical support and root cause analyses in handling manufacturing issues, deficiencies, concrete quality, etc.

Post-Manufacturing Services

We provide logistics, lifting, handling, installation planning, inspections, certifications, etc.

Training, Coaching, and Mentoring

You will receive customized training, team coaching on projects, and personal mentoring for your technical professionals.

Emergency Site Visits

Our engineers will attend job sites for conditional assessments and remediation plans.
